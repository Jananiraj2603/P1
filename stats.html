Programme Content / Core Subjects

B.Sc. Honours in Statistics — Core Subjects include:

Descriptive Statistics, Random Variables & Mathematical Expectations, Theoretical Discrete Distributions, Theoretical Continuous Distributions, Statistical Methods, Inferential Statistics, Sampling Techniques, Design and Analysis of Experiments, Numerical Analysis, Applied Statistics, Computational Statistics and R Programming, Operations Research (or) Statistical Quality Control, Operations Techniques (or) Applied Statistics – II, Advanced Operations Research (or) Actuarial Statistics, Multivariate Analysis (or) Linear Models & Applied Regression Analysis, Advanced Sampling Techniques (or) Stochastic Processes, Advanced Optimization Techniques (or) Advanced Actuarial Statistics, Advanced Design and Analysis of Experiments (or) Linear Algebra, Modern Inferential Statistics Practical (or) Applied Probability & Distributions.

M.Sc. Statistics — Core Subjects include:

Probability, Distribution Theory, Linear Algebra and Matrix Theory, Sampling Theory, Database Management Systems (DBMS), Statistical Inference – I (Theory of Estimation), Multivariate Analysis, Linear Models & Applied Regression Analysis, Stochastic Processes, Statistical Computing, Statistical Inference – II (Tests of Hypothesis), Design & Analysis of Experiments, Mathematical Programming, Statistical Quality Control & Reliability, Programming in R, Operations Research, Time Series Analysis & Forecasting Methods, Data Analysis using SPSS, Biostatistics / Econometrics (Internal Elective).
