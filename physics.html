The department of Physics was established in 2004. It offers M.Sc. Physics (Self-Finance) with an intake of 60 students. The department has a tradition of teaching to impart knowledge and skill-based quality education in theoretical, experimental and technological aspects. Students get training via general-physics and electronics experiments. The course is self-supporting and caters especially to socially and economically backward sections, many of whom are first-generation women learners. The department provides access to a high-end library for both students and faculty. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Vision: To reach young women students through education, foster self-reliance, leadership, and independence; and to empower them through quality education in physics. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
