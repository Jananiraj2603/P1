The department offers M.Sc. Applied Mathematics programme since 1984. The curriculum is designed under CBCS pattern to offer subjects that are job oriented as well as practically useful in different branches of Science and Technology. The programme emphasizes professional skill development for employment, empowerment, and emancipation.

Programmes

Programme Name Duration Regular Seats Payment Seats
M.Sc. Applied Mathematics 2 Years (4 Semesters) 35 + 4 (EWS) 06

Eligibility :

B.A. / B.Sc. Degree with Mathematics as one of the optional subjects, with 50% in Mathematics. Candidates who have studied Mathematics as an ancillary subject are not eligible.

Courses Content

Core Subjects :

Algebra, Real Analysis, Topology, Ordinary Differential Equations, Complex Analysis, C-Programming, Principles of Mechanics, Partial Differential Equations, Numerical Methods, Probability Theory.

Research Programmes

The Department is actively involved in Research Programmes on various thrust areas. These are offered Full-time and Part-time as per UGC guidelines.
