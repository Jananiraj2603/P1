The Department of Psychology was established in academic year 2020-2021 at SPMVV as a postgraduate department. It offers a 2-year M.Sc. programme in Clinical Psychology (Self-Finance), with specialization in Clinical Psychology and Counselling Psychology, focusing on handling pathological or abnormal behaviour and mental health issues. The programme includes biological, psycho-physiological, and social foundations of behaviour, personality, research methodology, developmental psychology, intensive practicum & internship. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be among the premier Departments of Clinical / Counselling Psychology through commitment and innovation in teaching, research and community services for promoting positive mental health and wellness. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Mission:

Eligibility

Any Sciences graduate is eligible. Also B.A. Psychology / B.Sc. Psychology / B.Sc. Home Science / Social Work (BSW) / Physiotherapy (BPT) / B.Sc. Nursing / B.Ed. with Psychology as one subject — with 50% average marks — are eligible for admission. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

Course Content – Core Subjects

The programme includes Introduction to Psychology, Psychological Research, Statistical Methods for Psychological Research, Understanding and Dealing with Psychological Disorders, Social Physiology, Counselling Psychology, Psychology of Individual Differences, Applied Social Psychology, Developmental Psychology, Clinical Psychology, and related practicum & internship. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Programme Outcomes

Upon completion, students are expected to develop understanding of clinical psychology and human behaviour, gain skills in counselling, therapeutic interventions, research methodology, and be equipped to work in mental-health services, counselling centers, community welfare, or further studies. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
