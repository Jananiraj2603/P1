The Department of Home Science offers two M.Sc courses; M.Sc Food and Nutrition Sciences and M.Sc Human Development and Family Studies, one P.G. Diploma course in preschool education (one year) and a certificate course in Child Care and Management (six months). These courses are applied in nature and are designed to train the students in Theory, Practice, Research and Extension in their field of study. All these courses have great relevance to the Institutions, Agencies, Organizations, Departments, Projects, Industries, Hospitals and Corporations working in the area of Health, Nutrition, Family, Community and Human Development and Management. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

M.Sc. Food & Nutrition Sciences — Specialisations & Course Content

Specialization A: Clinical Nutrition and Dietetics (CND)

Subjects include: Food Science, Human Nutrition and Dietetics, Applied Physiology, Nutrition & Health Interventions, Life-Style Management of Diseases, Communicative English, Advances in Nutrition; Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics I & II, Nutritional Biochemistry, Nutritional Status Assessment Methodologies, Food Standards, Quality & Safety, Computer Applications, Functional Foods, Nutrition during Emergencies & Disaster Management, Research Methodology & Statistics, Seminars/Panel Discussions, Gender Studies, Nutritional Counselling, Food Service Management, Food–Drug Interaction, Dissertation and 4-week in-plant Training. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Specialization B: Community Health and Nutrition (CHN)

Subjects include: Food Science, Nutrition during Lifespan – I & II, Nutrition Ecology & Health Education, Fundamentals of Nutrition, Health Interventions, Communicative English, Advances in Nutrition, Women & Reproductive Health, Nutritional Status Assessment, Food Standards & Safety, Computer Applications, Functional Foods, Management of Community Nutrition Problems, Nutrition during Emergencies & Disaster Management, Research Methodology & Statistics, Seminars/Panel Discussions, Gender Studies, Community Dietetics & Counselling, Food Service Management, Entrepreneurship Development, Dissertation and 4-week in-plant Training. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Specialization C: Food Science and Quality Control (FSQC)

Subjects include: Food Science, Food Chemistry, Functional Properties of Foods, Food Microbiology & Quality Evaluation, Communicative English, Advances in Nutrition, Food Analysis, Nutritional Biochemistry, Food Processing & Preservation, Food Standards, Quality & Safety, Computer Applications, Functional Foods, Food Toxicology, Community Nutrition, Research Methodology & Statistics, Seminars & Panel Discussions, Gender Studies, Food Product Development, Food Service Management, Food–Drug Interaction, Dissertation and 4-week in-plant Training. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

M.Sc. Human Development and Family Studies (HDFS) — Course Content

Course includes: Theories of Human Development, Life Span Development I & II & III, Techniques of Child Study, Early Childhood Education, Communicative English, Organization & Administration of Early Childhood Education Institutions, Children with Special Needs, Community Health & Nutrition Education, Communication Technology, Computer Applications, Participation in Preschools (2 weeks), Dynamics of Marriage and Family, Rehabilitation of Disabled Children, Research Methodology & Statistics, Seminars/Panel Discussions, Gender Studies, Project Work, Parenting, Counselling & Therapy, Personality Development, 4-week in-plant Training. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
