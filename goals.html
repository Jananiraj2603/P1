Goals

To fulfill our mission of “Emancipation of Women through Acquisition of Knowledge”, we have set major goals. These goals guide our planning strategies, which we review and update annually. We do our utmost to achieve each goal by safeguarding our established freedom to create an environment of excellence in teaching and research and by maintaining our standards as an institution of higher education in the region. Offer educational services, both general and professional, to women of all age groups, especially in Andhra Pradesh, to shape their lives and careers. Facilitate and maintain excellence in teaching. Advance knowledge through research and scholarly activity. Attract the most intellectually able staff and students. Secure and manage the resources that serve the interest of women through networking and collaboration. Disseminate knowledge to benefit and serve the people of Andhra Pradesh, India and International Community.
