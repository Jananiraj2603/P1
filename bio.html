Eligibility

For M.Sc. Biotechnology: Bachelor’s Degree with Chemistry/Biochemistry/Botany/Zoology/Biotechnology/Microbiology/Genetics/Medical Lab Technology/Applied Nutrition/Bioinformatics & Computer Applications, with minimum 50% in group aggregate marks. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

For Integrated Biotechnology (5-year): Intermediate with Bi.P.C / M.Bi.P.C with 50% in groups. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Programme Content

Core Subjects :

Microbiology, Cell Biology, Biomolecules, Molecular Genetics, Enzymology, Molecular Biology, Immunotechnology, Biophysical & Biochemical Techniques, Genetic Engineering, Microbial Technology, Plant Biotechnology, Animal Biotechnology, Nanobiotechnology, Biostatistics & Bioinformatics, Environmental Biotechnology. External Elective: Emerging Technologies in Biology. Project work in final year is mandatory. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
